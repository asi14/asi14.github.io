Many people love to use To-Do Lists, because it gives them a sense of direction of where they should be going in their lives. Here are my to-do lists for the next month, and for the long term.

Short-Term To-Do List
These are the things I have to do within the next few months, or what I'd prefer be done within the next few months.
  1. Pass the AP for AP European History: I'd like to get a 4 ~ 5 on this AP so I can send it in to colleges. While I do know I have other APs that I could try for (and hopefully compensate for this one), any one bad grade on an AP could be significantly problematic.
  2. Finish this Computer Science Project: I'd like to maintain my Computer Science average, hence I have to finish this project, and I have to finish it well.
  3. Pass the Regents Examination for Chemistry: Especially considering I've already dropped the SAT Subject Test for Chemistry, I need to at least pass the Regents so I can present at least something to colleges.

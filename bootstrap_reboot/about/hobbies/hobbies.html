Computer Science

About

Computer Science involves using computers to solve problems, at a minimum. Typically, Computer Science goes along with Computer Programming, as programming gives computer scientists increased flexibility over usage of a machine.

Why I Like It

One of the reasons why I like Computer Science is because it gives me a greater say in what I can do with computers. As cliche as this sounds, the computer was an innovative invention, and ripple-effects of the first modern computers (e.g. the Apple Computer) still show today, through the myarid number of achievements possible through computers. But one problem (at least for the typical consumer), is that they are restricted to what they understand, which usually means the GUI-- easy, simple, no weird language-thingies. Computer Science gives consumers (like myself) a greater ability to use computers, and opens the potential for computers to be used to solve even greater problems.
